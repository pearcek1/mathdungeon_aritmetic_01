Dividend Divisor Multiple Remainder

Example: GCD of 30 and 12

The GCD is the largest number that divides both numbers exactly. Euclid's method:

Dividend Divisor Multiple Remainder
30 12 2 R = 30 - (12 × 2) = 6
12 6 2 0

✅ The last divisor before remainder 0 is the GCD → 6

About Euclid

Euclid (~300 BCE) was a Greek mathematician who wrote Elements. He developed this algorithm to find the GCD efficiently. Divide the larger number by the smaller, then replace the pair with (smaller number, remainder) and repeat until remainder is 0.
